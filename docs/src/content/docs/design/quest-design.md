## Human Meadow (Levels 1-5)

**Quest Giver**: Guard Captain Marcus (150, 150)

| # | Quest | Objective | Rewards | Prereq |
|---|-------|-----------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Rat Extermination | Kill 3 Giant Rats | 25g, 50 XP | None |
| 2 | Wolf Hunter | Kill 5 Hungry Wolves | 50g, 100 XP | Quest 1 |
| 3 | Boar Bounty | Kill 4 Wild Boars | 60g, 120 XP | Quest 2 |
| 4 | Goblin Menace | Kill 3 Goblin Raiders | 75g, 150 XP | Quest 3 |
| 5 | Slime Cleanup | Kill 2 Mire Slimes | 60g, 120 XP | Quest 4 |
| 6 | Bandit Problem | Kill 3 Bandit Scouts | 100g, 200 XP | Quest 5 |
| 7 | The Alpha Threat | Kill 1 Diseased Wolf | 155g, 310 XP | Quest 6 |

**Zone Total**: 525 gold, 1,050 XP

### Mob Stats

| Mob | Level | HP | XP | Drops |
|-----|-------|----|----|-------|
| Giant Rat | 1 | 20 | 12 | Raw Meat, Small Bone |
| Wild Boar | 2 | 35 | 16 | Raw Meat, Scrap Leather |
| Hungry Wolf | 2 | 30 | 18 | Wolf Pelt, Raw Meat |
| Goblin Raider | 3 | 45 | 28 | Gold, Scrap Leather |
| Mire Slime | 3 | 40 | 24 | Gold |
| Bandit Scout | 4 | 55 | 35 | Gold, Light Leather |
| Diseased Wolf | 5 | 70 | 42 | Wolf Pelt, Light Leather |

### Strategy

- Start with Giant Rats (easiest, 20 HP)
- Buy an Iron Sword (100g) after completing the first 2 quests
- Equip Leather Vest (60g) before fighting Goblins
- Learn your class's Level 1 technique (10g) immediately

| 13 | Wilderness Survival | Challenge | ~225g, 450 XP |

**Zone Total**: ~1,175 gold, ~2,350 XP

**Mobs**: Forest Bears, Giant Spiders, Outlaws, Corrupted Beasts, Pack Leaders

**Strategy**:
- Upgrade to Steel Longsword (250g) or Chainmail (300g) before entering
- Learn Level 3 and Level 6 techniques
- Gather herbs and ores for profession leveling
- Complete "Wilderness Survival" to unlock Dark Forest

## Dark Forest (Levels 10-16)

**Quest Giver**: Priestess Selene

| # | Quest | Level | Rewards |
|---|-------|-------|---------|
| 14 | Shadows in the Dark | 11 | ~300g, 600 XP |
| 15 | Cult Cleansing | 12 | ~375g, 750 XP |
| 16 | Undead Purge | 13 | ~450g, 900 XP |
| 17 | Troll Slayer | 14 | ~525g, 1,050 XP |
| 18 | Golem Breaker | 15 | ~600g, 1,200 XP |
| 19 | The Necromancer's End | 16 (Boss) | ~750g, 1,500 XP |
| 20 | Master of the Dark Forest | Ultimate | ~675g, 1,350 XP |

**Zone Total**: ~3,675 gold, ~7,350 XP

**Mobs**: Shadow Creatures, Cultists, Undead Skeletons, Forest Trolls (regenerating HP), Ancient Golems (high DEF), **The Necromancer** (final boss)

**Boss Drops**: Necromancer's Essence (200g material), legendary crafting components

**Strategy**:
- Full Reinforced Hide armor set recommended (+35-45 total DEF)
- Masterwork weapons deal 50% more damage
- Stock Greater Health Potions (80g each)
- Learn all 4 class techniques before the Necromancer fight
- Use enchantment elixirs for the boss fight

## Wild Meadow (Levels 5-10)

**Quest Giver**: Ranger Thornwood

Quests involve bears, spiders, outlaws, corrupted creatures, and pack leaders. Completing "Wilderness Survival" unlocks the Dark Forest.

## Dark Forest (Levels 10-16)

**Quest Giver**: Priestess Selene

Quests involve shadow creatures, cultists, undead, trolls, golems, and culminate in the Necromancer boss fight. "Master of the Dark Forest" is the ultimate achievement.

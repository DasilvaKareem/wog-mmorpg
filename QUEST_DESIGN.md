## Reward Scaling Formula

**Gold Rewards**:
- Level 1-5: 25-150 gold per quest
- Level 5-10: 125-300 gold per quest
- Level 10-15: 275-1000 gold per quest

**XP Rewards**:
- Level 1-5: 50-300 XP per quest
- Level 5-10: 250-600 XP per quest
- Level 10-15: 550-2000 XP per quest

**Mob XP Values**:
- Formula: Level × 10 + 2 (base)
- Elites: Level × 20 + 20
- Bosses: Level × 30 + 60
